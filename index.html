Geographic Information Systems!

GIS can provide further help on data comprehension by providing visuals. It can used for entertainment, not just for educational purposes!

Quick Overview Learn More in Depth

Ways GIS is used in our daily lives.

Pokemon Go

Pokemon go has buildings marked and special landmarks for you to reach by using your current location. The app keeps track of where you caught your pokemons as well!.

Digital Maps

The most common use of a digital map such as Google maps and Apple maps is to help the user get from point A to point B. This require the app to have the users location and to constantly adapt to find the best route..

Oculus Rift

The oculous has to adjust to your real world setting space in order to create a safe border for the user while they are in game, This is an example of spacial scanning/ spatial analysis,

Find My

The Find My app, or any location finding app uses data of its wherabouts to pinpoint an object.
